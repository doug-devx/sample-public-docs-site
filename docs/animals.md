# Animal Info

## Why do cats purr?
Cats purr for multiple reasons:
- Expressing contentment
- Self-soothing during stress
- Potential healing mechanism
- Communication with humans and other cats

## How do penguins stay warm?
Penguins have remarkable adaptations:
- Thick layer of blubber
- Overlapping, waterproof feathers
- Huddle together in large groups
- Special circulatory system in legs

## Can animals predict natural disasters?
Some evidence suggests:
- Dogs detect earthquake vibrations
- Birds sense atmospheric pressure changes
- Elephants hear infrasound waves
- Unusual animal behavior can precede events

## Do elephants really never forget?
Elephants demonstrate:
- Exceptional long-term memory
- Recognize individuals after years
- Remember migration routes
- Complex social memory networks

## Why do dogs wag their tails?
Tail wagging communicates:
- Emotional state
- Excitement
- Anxiety
- Willingness to interact
- Subtle mood variations
